Chapter 1 — Restoration of the Lucky Few

He woke up. Nothing felt familiar. Not the room… not the people… not even himself.

“Who… are you?” His voice sounded strange to him. Two figures stared back — tall, dark-skinned, and smaller, messy-clothed, both tense and alert.

Daniel chuckled nervously. “You woke up! Cool… uh, I mean, wow. Really? You’re alive?” Rush grinned. “Finally. Thought we were keeping you dead.”

Div tried to remember… Nothing. No skills. No memories. Just instinct. “Why… are we here?” Daniel’s grin faded. “Because people want to make sure you stay gone.”
